# When is "AI" acceptable?

"ai" shall only be morally acceptable when it is a) a bare neuralesque construct, untrained; b) trained on legitimately & consensually acquired input data. these terms are non-negotiable

i do not, and shall not, have solutions for self-hosted "ai". you need to know that modern "ai" tech is not much progressed from the last few decades - what's actually changed is that the companies playing with it decided to break the social moral code and stole fuqtonnes of public, personal and private data and fed it into their neuralesque-net boxes; and since that produced a burst of "success", there's been additional push to produce hardware architectures optimised for neuralesque processing.
