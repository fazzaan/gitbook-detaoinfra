# Airoboros

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (5).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>by <a href="https://wallpapers.com/svg/ouroboros-dragon-circle-graphic-1vzi1m89iwyvci7b.html">simon.larson</a> </p></figcaption></figure>

What happens when there is nothing new left for the oroboros snake to consume? When all new input was produced by the snake itself? Humans always seek for the origin. If we continue along the path of always consuming what was produced by LLMs, and always producing by consuming what was made by LLMs, at some point there will be no new fresh input: that snake will consume itself. If you only think in terms of matter, then this seems to be no problem; but when you think in terms of energy consumption and life, the problem becomes immediately apparent.

The Corpus of human creativity was, and is, produced through exploration, curiosity, struggle, hardship, discovery, and obviously creativity. Skills develop through struggle, that is how an inability becomes an action, and the action becomes the skill, and then the skill becomes a habit and then it becomes a reflex. The struggle and the repetition are precisely the mechanism required for new knowledge to enter into your brain and body, and become a part of you. Moreover, answers produced by LLMs tend to look correct and may even appear actually correct, yet they are usually formed with some misunderstandings which are different or are differently conveyed than how a human with actual knowledge and understanding would have conveyed the information. I have tested LLMs many times by enquiring about topics I know about, by researching what it has told me, and by requesting it to produce materials on things I know about. The number of errors are untenable, yet its portrayal of incorrect info as fact is both astonishing and disturbing.

New inventions come to exist through someone or people creatively seeking and creating a solution to a problem. Generative AI is not creative it is consumptive and it processes through combination of existing ideas this means that truly new ideas will occur less frequently and most well-developed solutions ideas technology and inventions came not from one person but from a group of people together and or a lineage of people learning from each other. Each of those people learning from each other we're using their brains so you struggle challenge problem solving and creating to find new ways of solving a variety of problems in the world. When people use LLMs, generative AI, to think for them, they are robbing themselves — and indeed, are being robbed by companies like openai and anthropic, it must be said — are being robbed of the opportunity and experience of using their brains to discover and create new solutions, which also develops the brain's ability and capacity to process things.

Combine this process and this effect over multiple people and multiple generations and what will we have? We will have a situation in which the vast majority of people cannot think because they don't know how and haven't being trained to, and haven't had experience doing so or needing to — until, at some point, the majority of us are like this and there is nobody left to run the world or to think and create for themselves. This is arguably indiscernible from Armageddon.
