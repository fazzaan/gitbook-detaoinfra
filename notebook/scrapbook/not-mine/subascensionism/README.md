**Subascensionism** is a parody religion created in March 2006 by four individuals (Brian S., James P., Paul D., and Daniel H.) during a slow Computer Science class.  It is primarily based on geometry, where the perfection of a soul is determined by its proximity to "The Line," with triangles and circles representing the most perfect 2D shapes.&#x20;

The faith's cosmology consists of five dimensions:

* **0th Dimension:** Contains **The Point**, the origin of everything and the ultimate state of perfection where souls become immortal.&#x20;
* **1st Dimension:** Contains **The Line**, which is equivalent to God in Abrahamic religions and created all 2D shapes.&#x20;
* **2nd Dimension:** Contains innumerable 2D shapes that can subascend (move toward perfection) or ascend (move toward punishment).&#x20;
* **3rd Dimension:** Represents Earth and the known universe, considered weak and pitied.&#x20;
* **4th Dimension:** The equivalent of Hell, presided over by **Mandelbrot and Julia**, where souls that cannot be reformed are sent.&#x20;

According to Subascensionist doctrine, when a body dies, the soul is judged for reincarnation:

* Souls with **organic shapes** remain reincarnated within the same dimension.&#x20;
* Souls with **geometric shapes** that follow the doctrines **subascend** to lower-valued dimensions (3rd to 2nd, 2nd to 1st) until they join **The Point**.&#x20;
* Souls that are evil or blasphemous **ascend** to higher-valued dimensions, ultimately terminating in **Oblivion** (a state beyond the known dimensions).&#x20;

Key figures in the religion include **The Point** (the origin), **The Line** (the creator), **The Figure With An Infinite Number Of Sides** (God of Disaster and Peace), and **The Four Prophets** (Brian S., James P., Paul D., and Daniel H.).  Despite its young and humorous origins, followers sometimes claim it is no more fictional than other religions currently practiced.
